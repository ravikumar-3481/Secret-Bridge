About SecretBridge

Privacy shouldn't be a premium feature.

Built for journalists, corporate teams, and privacy advocates. We noticed a gap in the market: most "secure" file sharing services still hold the keys to your data.

SecretBridge changes the paradigm. By leveraging modern browser capabilities like the WebCrypto API and Compression Streams, your data is encrypted before it ever leaves your device.

  • No Accounts Required
  • No Software to Install
  • 100% Free to Use

Enterprise-Grade Features

Everything you need to stay secure.

Parallel Chunking

Large files are sliced into 5MB chunks and uploaded concurrently. Maximizes your bandwidth and ensures reliability without freezing your browser.

End-to-End Encrypted

AES-GCM 256-bit encryption occurs locally. The encryption key is derived from a PIN that only you and your recipient know. We never see it.

Digital Shredder

Files are stored in temporary memory buffers and are permanently shredded from our solid-state drives after 10 minutes or exactly one download.

Our Architecture

Why Trust SecretBridge?

Your data, your rules. We literally can't see your files.

Absolute Zero Knowledge

Data is encrypted on your device (Client-side) before transit. The server only receives mathematical garbage (ciphertext). We don't possess the decryption keys.

Native Browser Compression

Before encryption, payloads are compressed using the browser's native CompressionStream API. This reduces transit sizes by up to 40% without sending unencrypted data over the wire.

Guaranteed Auto-Burn

A 10-minute timer starts the moment your upload finishes. The file is permanently deleted from the database whether it has been downloaded or not. No backups, no recovery.

It's incredibly simple.

1

Upload & Lock
